Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals to choose from. When you click on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is random so dust off the old calculator and enjoy!
